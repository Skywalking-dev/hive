# Content Strategy for Dev Studios

Skywalking creates content to build authority in LATAM tech, attract clients who value craft, and convert readers who have real engineering problems to solve.

Every piece either builds SEO equity, builds reputation, or both. Nothing else justifies the effort.

## Before Planning

- **Goal this quarter:** Traffic? Leads? Brand? GitHub stars?
- **Primary audience:** Developers learning? CTOs evaluating vendors? Founders looking for a team?
- **Existing assets:** What's published? What performed? What can be repurposed?
- **Channels:** Blog, LinkedIn, X, email newsletter, GitHub?
- **Cadence capacity:** Realistic publishing frequency. 1 post/week done > 5/week planned and abandoned.
- **Language:** ES-LATAM for LATAM SEO. EN for global reach + higher DA backlinks.

## Content Pillars

1. **AI Tooling in Practice** — Real workflows with AI agents, LLMs, automation. Not theory.
2. **LATAM Tech Perspective** — Building products from Argentina. Constraints, creativity, ecosystem.
3. **Development Workflow** — How we work: methodology, agent delegation, testing, shipping.
4. **Open Source** — What we've published, why, what it solves. OSS as trust signal.
5. **Client Results** — Case studies: the problem, the stack, the outcome.

## Topic Selection

Good topics satisfy at least 2 of 3:
- **Search demand:** People are looking for this.
- **Differentiation:** We can say something more specific than what ranks today.
- **Conversion proximity:** Reader has a problem we solve.

ES-LATAM topics: lower competition, same audience in LATAM, compounds faster than EN.

## Content Calendar Template

| Week | Title | Pillar | Format | Channel | CTA | Owner |
|------|-------|--------|--------|---------|-----|-------|
| W1 | [topic] | [pillar] | Blog/Case study/Tutorial | Blog + LinkedIn | [action] | [who] |

Plan 4 weeks ahead minimum. One case study per month if client work permits.

## Repurposing Chain

```
Blog post (1500-2500 words)
→ 3 LinkedIn posts
→ 1 email newsletter
→ 1 WhatsApp broadcast (opt-in)
→ X thread
```

Never repost same text across channels. Adapt to platform norms.

## Format Principles

**Blog posts:** Problem → context → solution → result → CTA. Min 1 code block per post.

**Case studies:** Client problem → what we built → measurable result → quote. Real numbers or don't publish.

**Tutorials:** Working code. Tested. Explain the why. Link to repo.
