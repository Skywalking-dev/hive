# Social Content for Dev Studios

Skywalking's social presence: show real work, share honest takes, earn attention from the people we want to work with.

LinkedIn first. X second. Each channel has its own logic.

## Before Writing

- **Source material:** What is this based on? (Project shipped, lesson, opinion, data, tool released)
- **Voice:** Founder/personal or company page? (Founder = 3-5x more reach.)
- **Goal:** Brand awareness, engagement, or conversion?
- **Language:** EN for global, ES for LATAM community, or bilingual?
- **CTA:** Is there one? Not every post needs a hard CTA.

## LinkedIn

**Post anatomy:**

Line 1 (hook): Specific, not clever. "We shipped an AI agent system in 6 weeks. Here's what broke." beats "Thoughts on AI in 2026."

Lines 2-6 (context): What happened, what you learned, the constraint. Real details.

Lines 7-12 (insight): The thing someone else can use. Take a position.

Last line: Question for comments, link to repo, or strong closer. Not all three.

**Format rules:**
- 1300 chars optimal. 1800 max.
- Short paragraphs. 1-3 lines. White space = engagement.
- Max 3-5 hashtags. Relevant only.
- Links in first comment (LinkedIn suppresses outbound links).
- Post Tue-Thu, 9-11am local time.

**Post types that work:**
- "We built X. Here's how."
- "Lesson learned shipping X."
- "Contrarian take on [topic]."
- "Result: [metric]. What we did."
- "Tool/workflow I use daily."

**Avoid:**
- Motivational quotes without context
- "Excited to announce" without the announcement
- Engagement bait ("Comment YES if you agree")

## X/Twitter

**Threads:** Hook + tension → one idea per tweet → summary + CTA.

**Standalone:** Observations, hot takes, reactions. Under 240 chars.

**Frequency:** 2-3 posts/day. Threads 2x/week max.

## Bilingual Strategy

EN: global reach, developer communities, open source promotion.
ES: LATAM community, lower competition, client-facing.

Write each version independently. ES is not a translation of EN. Different idioms, different examples.

Neutral ES-LATAM for company page. Voseo OK on founder's personal account.

```
**X version:** [if bilingual]
**Variants:** [1 alt hook for A/B]
```

## Anti-Patterns

- Same text to LinkedIn and X verbatim
- "We're hiring!" as the entire post
- Listing features instead of outcomes
- Asking for likes/shares explicitly
- "Humbled and honored" as opener
