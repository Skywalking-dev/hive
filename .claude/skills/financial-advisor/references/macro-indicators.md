# Macro Economic Indicators Reference

## Tier 1: Must-Track (Check Weekly)

### Federal Reserve Interest Rate
- **Current**: 3.50-3.75% (held Jan 28, 2026)
- **Trajectory**: Three 25bp cuts preceded current hold
- **Market expectation**: Max 2 cuts in 2026, none before June
- **Impact**: Cuts = bullish stocks/crypto/bonds; Hikes = bearish
- **Track**: FOMC meeting schedule (8 meetings/year)

### CPI / Inflation
- **Headline CPI**: 2.7% annual (Dec 2025)
- **Core CPI**: 2.6% annual (lowest since Mar 2021)
- **Key components**: Shelter +0.4% monthly (biggest contributor), Food +0.7%, Energy +2.3%
- **Next release**: Feb 13, 2026
- **Impact**: High inflation → rate hikes → bearish. Declining → rate cuts → bullish.
- **Track**: Monthly BLS release

### Consumer Confidence
- **Conference Board**: 84.5 (Jan 2026) — 12-year low
- **UMich Sentiment**: 57.3 (Feb 2026) — third consecutive monthly increase
- **Key threshold**: Expectations Index below 80 = recession warning
- **Impact**: Low confidence → reduced spending → economic slowdown
- **Divergence note**: Confidence down but spending strong (Feb 2026)

### VIX (Volatility Index)
- **<15**: Complacent, potential buildup of risk
- **15-25**: Normal market conditions
- **25-30**: Elevated fear, buying opportunity potential
- **>30**: Panic/crisis levels — strong mean-reversion opportunity
- **>40**: Extreme (COVID, 2008 level) — generational buy signal
- **Impact**: Inverse correlation with S&P 500

### DXY (US Dollar Index)
- **Impact**: Dollar up = crypto/gold/emerging markets DOWN. Dollar down = UP.
- **Mechanism**: Strong dollar makes USD-denominated assets expensive for foreigners
- **Key correlations**: BTC -0.5 to -0.7 with DXY; Gold -0.4 to -0.6
- **Track**: Daily via TradingView or financial apps

## Tier 2: Important (Check Monthly)

### Yield Curve (2-10 Spread)
- **Normal**: 10Y > 2Y by 0.5-1.5%
- **Flat**: Near zero spread
- **Inverted**: 2Y > 10Y = recession warning (6-18 month lead time)
- **Current**: Track via FRED "T10Y2Y"
- **Historical accuracy**: Predicted every recession since 1955

### PMI (Purchasing Managers' Index)
- **>50**: Expansion
- **<50**: Contraction
- **50-55**: Moderate expansion (current)
- Two types: Manufacturing PMI and Services PMI
- Services PMI more important for US economy (70%+ GDP)
- **Track**: ISM monthly release

### Unemployment Rate
- **Current**: Track monthly (BLS)
- **NAIRU**: ~4.0% (natural rate)
- **Below 4%**: Tight labor market, potential wage inflation
- **Rising trend**: Recessionary signal
- **Sahm Rule**: If 3-month avg rises 0.5%+ above 12-month low = recession started

### GDP Growth
- **US**: Strong 2024-2025 performance
- **Global**: Moderate growth expected 2026
- **Argentina**: +4% GDP growth projected 2026
- **Impact**: Higher growth = bullish stocks; contraction = bearish
- **Track**: Quarterly BEA release

### M2 Money Supply
- **Expanding**: Inflationary; tends to boost asset prices (stocks, crypto, gold)
- **Contracting**: Deflationary; asset prices under pressure
- **2020-2021**: Massive M2 expansion → asset bubble
- **2022-2023**: M2 contraction → market correction
- **2024-2025**: Stabilizing
- **Predictive power**: 12-18 month lead on asset prices

## Crypto-Specific Indicators

### Fear & Greed Index
- **Source**: alternative.me
- **0-25**: Extreme fear (BUY signal)
- **25-45**: Fear
- **45-55**: Neutral
- **55-75**: Greed
- **75-100**: Extreme greed (SELL/profit-take signal)
- **Historical edge**: Buying at extreme fear outperforms

### BTC Dominance
- **Rising**: Risk-off, money flowing to BTC from alts
- **Falling**: Altseason starting (money flowing to alts)
- **Peak ~60%**: Historical altseason trigger
- **Current**: Track via CoinGecko or TradingView

### Funding Rates
- **Extreme positive**: Overleveraged longs → correction risk
- **Extreme negative**: Oversold → bounce potential
- **Track**: Coinglass.com or exchange dashboards

### Exchange Flows
- **Inflows to exchanges**: Selling pressure (bearish)
- **Outflows from exchanges**: Hodling (bullish)
- **Track**: CryptoQuant, Glassnode

### Stablecoin Flows
- **Stablecoin market cap growing**: New money entering crypto (bullish)
- **USDT/USDC minting**: Fresh capital inflow
- **Stablecoin dominance rising**: Risk-off within crypto

- **3rd-4th week**: Fed meeting (8x/year)
- **End of month**: Consumer confidence, GDP

- **Jan/Apr/Jul/Oct**: Earnings season (~2 weeks of major reports)
- **Quarterly**: GDP revisions

- **March**: Tax loss harvesting season ends

- **December**: Santa rally tendency, tax-loss harvesting
